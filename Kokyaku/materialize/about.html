KOKYAKU: meeting your client expectations

Tired of missing important appointments? Is it hard for you to keep up with small clients? Do you forget youdr deadlines? Is all of that ruining your reputation? We understand you.
Kokyaku is the new client manager that helps you keep track of your clients. With it, you can easily keep track of your deadlines, your client’s appointments, your client’s status and even any quick note you must not forget next time you meet them.
The idea is to help small business build better relationships with all of their clients, never letting go a single client, no matter how big or small.

Developed by students of Tecnologico de Monterrey campus Guadalajara, Kokyaku is a web application that aims to support SMEs in automating their agendas and schedules for customers who can be used regardless of the company's turnover.
